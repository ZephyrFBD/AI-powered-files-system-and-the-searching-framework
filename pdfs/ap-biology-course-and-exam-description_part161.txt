SYI-3.C.1
Segregation, independent assortment of 
chromosomes, and fertilization result in genetic

The chromosomal basis of inheritance 
provides an understanding of the pattern of 
transmission of genes from parent to offspring.

Certain human genetic disorders can 
be attributed to the inheritance of a 
single affected or mutated allele or 
specific chromosomal changes, such as 
nondisjunction.
