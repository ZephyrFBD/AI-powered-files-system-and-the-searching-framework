MEPHIST. But I am an instance to prove the contrary, For I tell thee I
am damn'd and now in hell.
FAUSTUS. Nay, an this be hell, I'll willingly be damn'd: What!
sleeping, eating, walking, and disputing! But, leaving this, let me have a
wife, The fairest maid in Germany; For I am wanton and lascivious, And
cannot live without a wife.
MEPHIST. Well, Faustus, thou shalt have a wife.
[MEPHISTOPHILIS fetches in a WOMAN-DEVIL.]
FAUSTUS. What sight is this?
MEPHIST. Now, Faustus, wilt thou have a wife?
FAUSTUS. Here's a hot whore, indeed:  no, I'll no wife.
