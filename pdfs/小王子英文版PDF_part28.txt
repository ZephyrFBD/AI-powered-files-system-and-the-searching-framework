So, too, she began very quickly to torment him
with her vanity­­ which was, if the truth be known, a
little difficult to deal with. One day, for instance, when

"Let the tigers come with their claws!"
"There are no tigers on my planet," the little prince
objected. "And, anyway, tigers do not eat weeds."
"I am not a weed," the flower replied, sweetly.
