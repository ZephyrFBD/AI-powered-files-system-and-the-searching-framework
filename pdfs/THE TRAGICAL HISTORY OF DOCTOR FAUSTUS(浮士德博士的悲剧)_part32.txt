FAUSTUS. Ay, go, accursed spirit, to ugly hell! 'Tis thou hast damn'd

Re-enter GOOD ANGEL and EVIL ANGEL.
EVIL ANGEL. Too late.
GOOD ANGEL. Never too late, if Faustus will repent.
EVIL ANGEL. If thou repent, devils will tear thee in pieces.
GOOD ANGEL. Repent, and they shall never raze thy skin. [Exeunt
ANGELS.]
FAUSTUS. O Christ, my Saviour, my Saviour Help to save distressed
Faustus' soul!
