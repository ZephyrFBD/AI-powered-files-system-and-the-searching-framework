"Do you know, it was I who found him his wife?" said Tiare suddenly, with a smile that spread all 
over her immense face.
"The cook?"
"No, Strickland."
"But he had one already."
"That is what he said, but I told him she was in England, and England is at the other end of the 
world."
"True," I replied.
