walked out of the house immediately after dinner, and the fact that Blanche had washed up the

deliberate. Her self-possession was frightening. A sudden pang seized him, and his knees felt so 
weak that he almost fell. He went back into the bedroom and threw himself on the bed. He cried 
out her name.
"Blanche. Blanche."
