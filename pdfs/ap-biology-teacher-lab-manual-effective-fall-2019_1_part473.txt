■ Learning Objectives 
•  IST-1.B: Describe the events that occur in the cell cycle.  
•  IST-1.C: Explain how mitosis results in the transmission of chromosomes from one 
generation to the next.  
•  IST-1.F: Explain how meiosis results in the transmission of chromosomes from one 
generation to the next.  
•  IST-1.G: Describe similarities and/or differences between the phases and outcomes of 
mitosis and meiosis.  
■ General Safety Precautions 
You must be careful when preparing specimens for viewing under the compound  
microscope. Always cover the cover slip with a scientific cleaning wipe, such as a  
Kimwipe, and press down using a pencil eraser.  
You should wear safety goggles or glasses and disposable gloves when handling the 
chemicals and razor blades in Parts 2 and 5. All materials should be disposed of properly 
as per your teacher’s instructions.
