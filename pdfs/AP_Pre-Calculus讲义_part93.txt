3.8 Addition and Subtraction Formula 
3.8.1 Addition and Subtraction Formula 
Formulas for sine:  
sin (s + t) = sin s cos t + cos s sin t 
sin (s − t )= sin s cos t − cos s sin t 
Formulas for cosine: 
cos (s + t) = cos s cos t − sin s sin t 
cos (s − t) = cos s cos t + sin s sin t
