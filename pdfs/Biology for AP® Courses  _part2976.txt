Common Problems with Elimination
Diarrhea and constipation are some of the most common health concerns that affect digestion. Constipation is a condition
where the feces are hardened because of excess water removal in the colon. In contrast, if enough water is not removed from
the feces, it results in diarrhea. Many bacteria, including the ones that cause cholera, affect the proteins involved in water
reabsorption in the colon and result in excessive diarrhea.
