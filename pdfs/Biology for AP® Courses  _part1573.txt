a.
DNA is made up of two strands that are twisted
around each other to form a helix. Adenine pairs
up with thymine and cytosine pairs with
guanine. The two strands are anti-parallel in
nature; that is, the 3’ end of one strand faces the
5’ end of other strand. Sugar, phosphate and
nitrogenous bases contribute to the DNA
structure.
