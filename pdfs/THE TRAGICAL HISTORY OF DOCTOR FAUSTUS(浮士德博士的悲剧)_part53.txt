FAUSTUS. I thank you, sir. [Snatches the dish.]
POPE. How now! who snatch'd the meat from me? Villains, why
speak you not?-- My good Lord Archbishop, here's a most dainty dish Was
sent me from a cardinal in France.
FAUSTUS. I'll have that too. [Snatches the dish.]
POPE. What Lollards do attend our holiness, That we receive
such<133> great indignity? Fetch me some wine.
FAUSTUS. Ay, pray, do, for Faustus is a-dry.
POPE. Lord Raymond, I drink unto your grace.
FAUSTUS. I pledge your grace. [Snatches the cup.]
