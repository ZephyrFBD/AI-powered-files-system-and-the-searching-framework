yet there is something mysterious in the sight which haunts and troubles you. You feel I know not 
what primitive force which repels and yet fascinates you. Here all the decencies of civilisation are 
swept away, and you feel that men are face to face with a sombre reality. There is an atmosphere 
that is at once intense and tragic.
