Questions & Methods questions ask you to determine the methods best suited for an experiment. You may be asked
to develop a testable question to prove or disprove an idea and/or develop hypotheses for an experiment. You may
also be asked to identify experimental design components for experiments, such as independent and dependent
variables and controls. You may be asked to propose the next steps for an experiment based on the methods used or
the data collected.
