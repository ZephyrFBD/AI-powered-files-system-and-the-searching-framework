Connection for AP® Courses
The information in this section does not directly apply to concepts and content required for AP®. However, as a student
of biology and traveler through the amazing world of Earth’s living organisms, you have learned why it is important to
protect ecosystems and preserve species. When we think of saving species through conservation efforts, we typically target
endangered animals—despite the fact that most of the world’s food supply comes from plants, not animals. The challenges
associated with preserving biodiversity must be met with understanding biodiversity itself, changes in human behavior and
beliefs, and various preservation strategies.
