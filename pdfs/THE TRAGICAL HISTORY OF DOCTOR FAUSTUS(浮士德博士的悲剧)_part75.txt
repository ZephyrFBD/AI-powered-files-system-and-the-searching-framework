FAUSTUS. What art thou, Faustus, but a man condemn'd to die? Thy
fatal time draws to a final end; Despair doth drive distrust into my
thoughts: Confound these passions with a quiet sleep: Tush, Christ did call
the thief upon the Cross; Then rest thee, Faustus, quiet in conceit. [He sits
