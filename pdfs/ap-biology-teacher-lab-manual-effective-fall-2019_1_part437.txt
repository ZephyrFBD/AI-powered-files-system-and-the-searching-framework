■ Safety and Housekeeping 
This laboratory investigation, especially Parts 1, 3, and 4, has a few safety concerns. 
Remind students to wear gloves and safety goggles or glasses when handling the 
chemicals and razor blades in Parts 2 and 5. To avoid injuries, students should use a 
pencil eraser rather than their thumbs to press down on the cover slips.
