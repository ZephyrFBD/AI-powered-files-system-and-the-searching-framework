Chemistry of Life
ELEMENTS
Although life-forms exist in many diverse forms, they all have one thing in common: they are all made up of matter
that is exchanged with the environment to grow, reproduce, and maintain organization. Matter is made up of
elements. Elements, by definition, are substances that cannot be broken down into simpler substances by chemical
means.
