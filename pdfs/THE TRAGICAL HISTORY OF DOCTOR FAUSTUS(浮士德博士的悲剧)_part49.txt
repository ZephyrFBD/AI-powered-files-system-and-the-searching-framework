POPE. It is enough.  Here, take him to your charge, And bear him
straight to Ponte<125> Angelo, And in the strongest tower enclose him
fast. To-morrow, sitting in our consistory, With all our college of grave
cardinals, We will determine of his life or death. Here, take his<126>
triple crown along with you, And leave it in the church's treasury. Make
haste again, my good Lord Cardinals, And take our blessing apostolical.
