You don’t need a perfectly written response to get the maximum number of points, either, so don’t waste your time
with fancy introductions like “It was the best of experiments, it was the worst of experiments.” Just leap right into
your response. Don’t even sweat grammar or spelling errors; these can hurt you only if they’re so bad that they
seriously impair the reader’s ability to understand you.
