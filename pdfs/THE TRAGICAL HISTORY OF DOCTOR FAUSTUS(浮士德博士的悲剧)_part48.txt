MEPHIST. Now tell me, Faustus, are we not fitted well?
FAUSTUS. Yes, Mephistophilis; and two such cardinals Ne'er serv'd a
holy Pope as we shall do. But, whilst they sleep within the consistory, Let
us salute his reverend fatherhood.
RAYMOND. Behold, my lord, the Cardinals are return'd.

holy council there decreed Concerning Bruno and the Emperor, In
quittance of their late conspiracy Against our state and papal dignity?
