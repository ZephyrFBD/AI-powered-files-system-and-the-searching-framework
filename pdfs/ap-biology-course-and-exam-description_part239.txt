ESSENTIAL KNOWLEDGE
EVO-1.O.1
An adaptation is a genetic variation that is 
favored by selection and is manifested as a trait 
that provides an advantage to an organism in a 
particular environment.
EVO-1.O.2
Mutations are random and are not directed by 
specific environmental pressures.
ESSENTIAL KNOWLEDGE
SYI-2.A.1
The intentional or unintentional introduction 
of an invasive species can allow the species 
to exploit a new niche free of predators or 
competitors or to outcompete other organisms 
for resources.
