"But there is nobody here to judge!"
"We do not know that," the king said to him. "I have not yet made a complete tour of
my kingdom. I am very old. There is no room here for a carriage. And it tires me to walk."
"Oh, but I have looked already!" said the little prince, turning around to give one
more glance to the other side of the planet. On that side, as on this, there was nobody at
all...
"Then you shall judge yourself," the king answered. "that is the most difficult thing of
all. It is much more difficult to judge oneself than to judge others. If you succeed in
judging yourself rightly, then you are indeed a man of true wisdom."
"Yes," said the little prince, "but I can judge myself anywhere. I do not need to live on
this planet.
