"When a woman loves you she's not satisfied until she possesses your soul. Because she's weak, 
she has a rage for domination, and nothing less will satisfy her. She has a small mind, and she 
resents the abstract which she is unable to grasp. She is occupied with material things, and she is 
jealous of the ideal. The soul of man wanders through the uttermost regions of the universe, and 
she seeks to imprison it in the circle of her account-book. Do you remember my wife? I saw 
Blanche little by little trying all her tricks. With infinite patience she prepared to snare me and bind 
me. She wanted to bring me down to her level; she cared nothing for me, she only wanted me to be 
hers. She was willing to do everything in the world for me except the one thing I wanted: to leave 
me alone."
