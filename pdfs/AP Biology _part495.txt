INTRODUCTION
cology is the study of the interactions of organisms with their
physical environment and with each other. Here is some introductory
vocabulary for the topic.
A population is a group of individuals of one species living in one
area that have the ability to interbreed and interact with each other.
A community consists of all the organisms living in one area.
