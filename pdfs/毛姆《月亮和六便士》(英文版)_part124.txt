She flushed a little, embarrassed by the passion in his tone. His letters had told me that he was very 
much in love with his wife, and I saw that he could hardly take his eyes off her. I could not tell if 
she loved him. Poor pantaloon, he was not an object to excite love, but the smile in her eyes was 
affectionate, and it was possible that her reserve concealed a very deep feeling. She was not the 
ravishing creature that his love-sick fancy saw, but she had a grave comeliness. She was rather tall, 
and her gray dress, simple and quite well-cut, did not hide the fact that her figure was beautiful. It
