Bubonic plague caused by Yersinia pestis was a
pandemic that occurred in the 14th century. In
modern times, there are only about 100 cases of
bubonic plague each year. The bacterium
responds well to modern antibiotics.
b.
Bubonic plague caused by Yersinia
enterocolitica was a pandemic that occurred in
the 14th century. In modern times, there are
about 1,000 to 3,000 cases of bubonic plague
each year. The bacterium responds well to
modern antibiotics.
c.
Pneumonic plague caused by Yersinia pestis was
a pandemic that occurred in the 14th century. In
modern times, there are about 1,000 to 3,000
cases of pneumonic plague each year. The
bacterium responds well to modern antibiotics.
