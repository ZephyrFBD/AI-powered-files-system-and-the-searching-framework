TYPES OF DATA
Data can be quantitative (based on numbers or amounts that can be measured or counted) or qualitative (data that
is descriptive, subjective, or difficult to measure). For example, behavioral observations are qualitative. Most data
on the AP Biology Exam will be quantitative, with either counts or measurements.
Count Data
