I had never heard Strickland speak so much at one time. He spoke with a passion of indignation. 
But neither here nor elsewhere do I pretend to give his exact words; his vocabulary was small, and 
he had no gift for framing sentences, so that one had to piece his meaning together out of 
interjections, the expression of his face, gestures and hackneyed phrases.
