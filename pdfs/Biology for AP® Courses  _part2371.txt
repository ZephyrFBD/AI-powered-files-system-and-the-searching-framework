Viruses cause a variety of diseases in animals, including humans, ranging from the common cold to potentially fatal illnesses
like meningitis (Figure 21.14). These diseases can be treated by antiviral drugs or by vaccines, but some viruses are capable
of both avoiding the immune response and mutating to become resistant to antiviral drugs.
