BELZEBUB. Thou shouldst not think on God.
LUCIFER. Think of the devil.
BELZEBUB. And his dam too.
FAUSTUS. Nor will Faustus henceforth:  pardon him for this, And
Faustus vows never to look to heaven.
LUCIFER. So shalt thou shew thyself an obedient servant, And we
will highly gratify thee for it.
BELZEBUB. Faustus, we are come from hell in person to shew thee
some pastime:  sit down, and thou shalt behold the Seven Deadly Sins
appear to thee in their own proper shapes and likeness.
FAUSTUS. That sight will be as pleasant unto me, As Paradise was to
Adam the first day Of his creation.
LUCIFER. Talk not of Paradise or creation; but mark the show.-- Go,
Mephistophilis, and<79> fetch them in.
MEPHISTOPHILIS brings in the SEVEN DEADLY SINS.
BELZEBUB. Now, Faustus, question them of their names and
dispositions.
FAUSTUS. That shall I soon.--What art thou, the<80> first?
