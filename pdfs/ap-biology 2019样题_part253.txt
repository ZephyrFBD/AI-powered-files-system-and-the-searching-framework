chemical burns
Bullet Use of safety goggles, eye washes, body showers, 
fire blankets, and fire extinguishers
Bullet Safe handling of glassware, hot plates, burners and 
other heating devices, and electrical equipment
Bullet Proper interpretation of Material Safety Data Sheets 
(MSDS) and hazard warning labels
Bullet Proper use and reuse practices (including proper 
labeling of interim containers) for reagent bottles
