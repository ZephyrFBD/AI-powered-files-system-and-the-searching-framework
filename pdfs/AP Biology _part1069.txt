FAD (flavin adenine dinucleotide) A coenzyme that carries protons or
electrons from glycolysis and the Krebs cycle to the electron transport
chain.
Fermentation A synonym for anaerobic respiration. The anaerobic
breakdown of glucose into pyruvic acid.
Fixed action pattern An innate, highly stereotypic behavior, which when
begun, is continued to completion, no matter how useless.
Flagella The tail-like structure that propels some single-celled organisms.
Flagella consist of microtubules.
Follicle-stimulating hormone (FSH) A hormone released from the
anterior pituitary that stimulates the ovarian follicle.
Food chain The pathway along which food is transferred from one trophic
level to the next.
