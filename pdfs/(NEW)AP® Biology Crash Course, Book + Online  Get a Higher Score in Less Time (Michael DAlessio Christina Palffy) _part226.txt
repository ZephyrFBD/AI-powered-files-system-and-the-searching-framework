•
Eye color is sex-linked in Drosophila melanogaster. Assume red eye (Xw+) is dominant to white
eye (Xw). Cross pure breeding red eye female to a white eye male.
•
F1 cross: Xw+Xw+ x XwY → All progeny red eye: females are carrier Xw+Xw and male Xw+Y
•
F2 cross: Xw+ Xw x Xw+Y → Progeny 1 Xw+Xw, 1 Xw+Xw+, 1 XwY, 1 XwY (all females have red
eyes, ½ males have red eyes, and ½ males have white eyes).
