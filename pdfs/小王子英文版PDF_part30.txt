And he continued his confidences:
"The fact is that I did not know how to understand anything! I ought to have judged
by deeds and not by words. She cast her fragrance and her radiance over me. I ought
never to have run away from her... I ought to have guessed all the affection that lay
behind her poor little strategems. Flowers are so inconsistent! But I was too young to
know how to love her..."
