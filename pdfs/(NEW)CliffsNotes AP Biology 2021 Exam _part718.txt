Short Questions
1. A species of fly that does not bite or sting has alternating yellow and black stripes on its body.
 
In two or three sentences, explain the value these markings bring to these harmless flies.
 
2. A keystone species has a strong influence on the health of a community.
Are you more likely to find a keystone species in a large, complex community or in a small, simple community?
Explain in two or three sentences.
