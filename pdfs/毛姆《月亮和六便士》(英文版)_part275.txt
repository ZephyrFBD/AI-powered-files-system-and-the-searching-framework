vaguely some spiritual meaning in material things that was so strange that he could only suggest it 
with halting symbols. It was as though he found in the chaos of the universe a new pattern, and 
were attempting clumsily, with anguish of soul, to set it down. I saw a tormented spirit striving for 
the release of expression.
